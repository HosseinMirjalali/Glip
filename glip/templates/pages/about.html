Twitch login process

You login on Twitch website and your credentials are handled by Twitch, not this website. Once you successfully log in on Twitch, I will receive from Twitch, your username (your public name), your email, and your avatar. They all can be seen on My Profile after you log in. Please, make sure you are in Twitch's website when you are logging in, by checking the url (https://www.twitch.tv/ or https://id.twitch.tv/)

Clips For You

There you will find clips that are chosen for you with regards to who you follow on Twitch, and which games you follow on Glip. I query the Twitch API to see the streamers you follow, then I query Twitch for each of your followed games and get top clips of that game. Lastly, I will choose, among all the clips that Twitch API has given me, the ones whose streams you follow. As an example, you follow Shroud, XQC, and Asmongold, and you have followed Valorant, Just Chatting, and World of Warcraft on Games on Glip. In Clips For You , you will only see Shroud, XQC, and Asmongold's clips if they're among the top X clips of aforementioned games.

If something new is lowering the quality of your experience, please let me know.
