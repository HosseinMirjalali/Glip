Twitch login process

You login on Twitch website and your credentials are handled by Twitch, not this website. Once you successfully log in on Twitch, I will receive from Twitch, your username (your public name), your email, and your avatar. They all can be seen on My Profile after you log in. Please, make sure you are in Twitch's website when you are logging in, by checking the url (https://www.twitch.tv/ or https://id.twitch.tv/)

Work in Progress

This website is still a work in progress. You will notice differences if you use the website regularly, since I add new features, update visuals, etc. on a daily basis. If something new is lowering the quality of your experience, please let me know. The cost of servers are currently all paid from my own pocket, so you might not be able to access the website, since I might shut them down to make the costs manageable.

Clips For You

There you will find clips that are chosen for you with regards to who you follow on Twitch, and which games you follow on Glip. I query the Twitch API to see the streamers you follow, then I query Twitch for each of your followed games and get top clips of that game. Lastly, I will choose, among all the clips that Twitch API has given me, the ones whose streams you follow. As an example, you follow Shroud, XQC, and Asmongold, and you have followed Valorant, Just Chatting, and World of Warcraft on Games on Glip. In Clips For You , you will only see Shroud, XQC, and Asmongold's clips if they're among the top X clips of aforementioned games.

Channels You Follow

See all the streamers you have followed on Twitch. This is fetched from Twitch API and is publicly available to anyone who can use Twitch API. The more streamers you follow, the more clips you see in Clips For You. You can watch their top clips (up to 100) from the past 24 hours by clicking on icon. You will see a Live icon if the streamer is online, and by clicking on that or icon, you will be redirected to their Twitch page.

Twitch Games

In this page you will see the current top 100 games on Twitch based on their view count. You can follow a game to see the clips of that game in Clips For You. If at any point, you don't want to see that game's clips anymore, you can always unfollow them. You can watch each game's top 100 clips from the past 24 hours by clicking on icon. Also, by clicking on icon, you will be redirected to that game's Twitch page.
